Information Systems is one of the departments of
College of Computer Science.



The Computer Information Systems major aims
to develop a set of foundations and rules that lead to
improving the methods of software design in a way that meets
the needs of users, and it is not concerned with writing the program itself
but rather concerned with improving the process of developing
the program development, and taking into account the ability
to easily develop the program later.

An Information Systems graduate can work in:


-Systems Designer
-Systems Consultant
-Programmer
-Project Manager
-Academic
-Database Design
